Draft schedule (pdf)

This year’s meeting will feature a discussion forum organized around a theme of great interest to CTC members: “Comparing cross designs for systems genetics.” The purpose is to generate lively discussion about the relative advantages of competing approaches for revealing different genetic architectures. The discussion will be facilitated by a few individuals with intimate knowledge of this area.
