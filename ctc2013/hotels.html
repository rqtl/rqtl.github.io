Hotels

Room blocks have been arranged at the following two locations, each a few minutes’ walk from the UW–Madison Memorial Union.

Hotel accommodations are not included in the registration fee. Please make your own reservations.

Lowell Center
610 Langdon Street
Madison, WI 53706
Phone: 866-301-1753 or 608-256-2621 (Refer to group code: traitcom)
Email: lowelll@ecc.uwex.edu
Online reservations

The Lowell Center is offering guest room rates of $89.00/night single for a standard room and $105.00/night single for a deluxe room. An expanded continental breakfast and high speed wireless internet is included in the room rate. This is a non-smoking facility. Guest parking for one vehicle is available on-site for $8 per night, or off-site (three blocks away) at no charge. Parking reservations should be made in advance of your visit, and reservations are based on availability.

Please make your reservations prior to 29 April 2013.

The Campus Inn
601 Langdon Street
Madison, WI 53703
Phone: 800-589-6285 (Refer to group number: 118284)
Online Reservations

The Dahlmann Campus Inn is offering guest room rates of $115/night single or double room, plus 14.5% tax. A continental breakfast, appetizers and desserts each evening, Dane county Regional Airport transportation (round trip via Madison Taxi), on-site parking, and high speed wireless internet are included in the room rate.

Please make your reservations prior to 28 April 2013.
